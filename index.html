About

IT Department ||| PCE

Poornima College of Engineering, also known as PCE, Jaipur is a private institution established in 2000 and is affiliated with Rajasthan Technical University. The institute is a part of the Poornima Group of Colleges (PGC) and is accredited by NBA. The student forum of PCE, namely the PCE IETE Student Forum is recognised as the most active ISF by IETE Rajasthan Center. Poornima College of Engineering is an 'Autonomous' institution, approved by the RTU, AICTE, and the UGC.Poornima College of Engineering was established in 2000 with the aim of imparting pragmatic technical education. In its magnificent journey of 22 years, PCE has set benchmarks and reached new pinnacles in engineering education with dedication, perseverance and devotion. PCE is spearheading its outstanding voyage with the motto ‘Success is not a destination, it’s a journey’. Poornima College of Engineering, Jaipur has been consistently ranked 2nd in QIV Ranking of Rajasthan Technical University, Kota, from the last five academic years. The QIV Ranking is based primarily upon Academics, University results, Placements & Alumni.
